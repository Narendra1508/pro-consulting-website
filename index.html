About PRO Consulting Engineers & Builders

PRO Consulting Engineers & Builders is a Planning, Designing and Construction Company with a team of engineers established in the year 2019 with an aim of providing good quality budget friendly homes.

Since 2019 we have successfully completed several residential projects across Bangalore, Bidadi, Ramnagar, Hassan and Shimoga. Our experienced team ensures every project meets the highest standards while maintaining cost-effectiveness.
